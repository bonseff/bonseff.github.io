Change


I work with people every day who jump in, testing their mettle. I love it… Change is where brilliance is born and thrives.

Hopelessly curious, relentlessly relentless


Applying change is something I can’t resist. It’s in my blood. It’s an energy form – one that I hope to transfer to you.

Obsessed with innovation and the art of applying it


I could not resist the temptation of taking apart household appliances. Most often I put them back together. Other times… Sorry, Mom!

About

Summary

I am a designer, strategist, researcher, leader, mentor, and storyteller dedicated to fostering innovation and delivering human-centered solutions that drive business success.

As a UX Strategist and Design Lead at Neighborly, I leverage over 20 years of experience in UX design and management to create innovative, user-centered SaaS products for the home services industry. I bring ideas to life by ensuring seamless brand integration across consumer journeys.
