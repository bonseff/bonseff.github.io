The Challenge

HCK2 is a marketing communications agency based in Addison, TX, specializing in creative advertising, design and branding, social media and digital communication, public relations, and web and interactive development.

As the Interactive Creative Director at HCK2, I had the privilege of working closely with an exceptional in-house development team. Over the course of a decade, we collaborated to deliver a wide range of digital solutions for local, regional, and national brands. Our work spanned various industries and included everything from innovative web designs to comprehensive digital marketing campaigns.

During my time at HCK2, I played a key role in developing and executing creative strategies that seamlessly integrated online and offline channels. This included not only designing visually engaging websites and interactive experiences but also ensuring that these digital solutions aligned with broader branding and communication goals. Our team's efforts consistently resulted in impactful, award-winning projects that enhanced brand visibility and engagement for our clients.
